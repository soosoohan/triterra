트리테라 (Triterra) = 삼각형 (Triangle) + 땅,세계(Terra:라틴어)

Connect the Dots Triangle.

Triterra = Triangle + Terra (Latin for “land” or “earth”)

📋 Game Rules

  • Two players take turns drawing lines
  • Complete a triangle to score and take another turn
  • Player with the most triangles wins!
  • Start the game with just one click!
